Intresting Facts About camel

Intresting Facts About chameleon

  • Most chameleons have a prehensile tail that they use to wrap around tree branches.
  • Chameleons live in Madagascar, Africa, Spain, Portugal and Asia in rain forests, savannas
  • According to the Integrated Taxonomic Information System (ITIS), there are 171 species of chameleons
